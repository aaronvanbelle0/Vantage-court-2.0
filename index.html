FAQ

Common questions.

How much does a court cost? +

It depends on size, site conditions, and surface choice. A backyard pickleball court typically runs $25,000-$50,000. A full tennis court is more. We give you a real number after we visit the site — no ballpark quotes over the phone.

How long does it take? +

A new build is usually 2-4 weeks from start to finish, weather permitting. A resurface job runs 3-7 days. We schedule around dry windows — Pacific Northwest weather doesn't always cooperate.

Do I need a permit? +

Usually yes for new builds, depending on your county and lot. We've done jobs in every county we serve and can help you through the permit process.

How long will my court last? +

The slab itself should last 20+ years. The acrylic surface usually needs a recoat every 5-8 years, depending on use and exposure. Proper drainage and moss prevention make a big difference in this climate.

What areas do you serve? +

Whatcom, Skagit, Snohomish, Island, and San Juan Counties. If you're nearby and not sure, give us a call.

Do you offer financing? +

We can point you toward financing partners on request, but we don't directly offer financing. Most homeowners use a HELOC, home improvement loan, or pay in milestones tied to project stages.
