What clients ask before they call.

How much does a tennis or pickleball court cost in Washington?

+

Ranges typically fall between $35,000 for backyard pickleball to $120,000+ for full-size cushioned tennis courts, depending on site prep and drainage.

How long does court resurfacing take?

+

Resurfacing usually takes 5–8 working days. It requires specific temperature and dry weather windows for the acrylic to cure properly.

Do you handle residential and commercial projects?

+

Yes. We work with private estate owners, HOAs, athletic clubs, and city parks across Northwest Washington.
